Who replied — and who didn't — in the PSZAER entity consultation

Important caveat. The consultation documents do not enumerate the full universe of bodies invited. The Government (art. 48.º RJIGT consultation, opened 5 May 2026) describes the consulted universe only by category — sectoral bodies, the five CCDRs, central-administration services, intermunicipal entities, municipal associations and affected municipalities — and states that any entity silent within 20 days is deemed to have "nothing to oppose". Non-responders were therefore never individually named or counted. So a complete "did not reply" list cannot be derived from the documents. The only hard data is the page-8 table of analise-pareceres.pdf ("Entidades — Sentido do Parecer Emitido"), which names 37 engaged entities. Source: /docs/analise-pareceres.pdf, p. 8.

Explicitly recorded as NOT having issued an opinion

Administração Central do Sistema de Saúde, I.P. (ACSS)
The only one of the 37 named entities marked "Não emitiu parecer" in the page-8 table. Every other listed body issued some opinion or contribution.

Two that replied but said next to nothing

  • Município de Penacova — no written opinion; geographic contribution only
  • Município de Loulé — "no direct ZAER incidence"
